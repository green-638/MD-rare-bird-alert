About this website

This websites aims to address the shortcomings of eBird's Rare Bird Alerts by enabling hotspot-specific alerts and custom alert frequency.

Currently, only Maryland counties and hotspots are supported. Future updates may add other states.

All data is accessed through the eBird.org API
